About Me

I am an Emerging Business Analyst with internship and freelance experience across finance and service-based domains. I specialize in bridging the gap between business needs and technical solutions, ensuring that products deliver real value to end users.

My core skills include requirement gathering, FRD/BRD documentation, wireframing, data visualization with Power BI, and client communication. I am proficient with tools such as Figma for UI/UX design and collaborate effectively with developers to ensure smooth project delivery.
